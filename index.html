WHAT IS ROCKET LEAGUE?

Think soccer. Think rocket-powered cars. Now combine those two thoughts. Rocket League. The sequal to Supersonic Acrobatic Rocket-Powered Battle Cars brings you high-flying, fast-paced gameplay with one simple rule: score the ball in the goal. With an incredibly realistic physics engine, Rocket Leauge brings you exciting and mind-blowing gameplay with fully customizable vehicles for a truly unique experience.
